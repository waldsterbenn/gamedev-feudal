# Obsidian Entertainment

## Overview

- Major game developer owned by Microsoft/Xbox Game Studios
- Known for deep narrative RPGs: Fallout: New Vegas, Pillars of Eternity, The Outer Worlds
- Created [[Pentiment]] (2022) — a unique medieval narrative RPG set in 16th-century Bavaria
- Pentiment won numerous narrative and writing awards (Metacritic 87/100)

## Design Significance

Pentiment demonstrates the power of grounding player choice in authentic social dynamics and class distinctions. The game's art direction, inspired by medieval manuscripts, shows the value of thematic consistency.
