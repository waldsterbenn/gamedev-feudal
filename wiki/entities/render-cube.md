# Render Cube

## Overview

- ~15-person development team
- Best known for: [[Medieval Dynasty]] (2021, 1.5M+ copies during EA phase)
- Genre focus: Survival, RPG, City Builder
- Published by Toplitz Productions

## Design Significance

Render Cube proved that the "medieval life" fantasy is underserved. Players want to *live* in a medieval world, not just manage it abstractly. Their small-team success story is relevant for [[feudal]] project scope planning.
