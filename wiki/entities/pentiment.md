# Pentiment

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Obsidian Entertainment]]
- **Publisher**: Xbox Game Studios
- **Release**: November 2022 (PC, Xbox), February 2024 (Switch, PS4, PS5)
- **Genre**: Narrative RPG, Adventure
- **Metacritic**: 87/100 — Won numerous narrative and writing awards
- **Sales**: Included with Xbox Game Pass; no standalone sales disclosed

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Deep exploration of medieval class structure — peasants, clergy, nobility
- 16th-century Bavarian town setting at the intersection of feudalism and renaissance
- Church hierarchy and monastic life
- Social dynamics between serfs, free townsfolk, and nobility
- Historical events — [[peasant revolts]], religious upheaval, printing press revolution
- Player choices shaped by class background and relationships

## Design Significance

**What to steal**: Class dynamics, narrative depth, authentic social depiction
**What to avoid**: No gameplay simulation (pure narrative)

Extraordinary storytelling and art direction inspired by medieval manuscripts. The game demonstrates the power of grounding player choice in authentic social dynamics.
