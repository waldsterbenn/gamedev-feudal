# Going Medieval

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Foxy Voxel]]
- **Publisher**: The Irregular Corporation
- **Release**: June 2021 (Early Access)
- **Genre**: Colony Simulation, Survival, City Builder
- **Metacritic**: Predominantly positive on Steam (~85%)
- **Sales**: Over 300,000 copies in EA within first year

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Vertical fortress building in 3D
- Settler management with job specialization and hierarchies
- Feudal labor organization — assigning roles, managing moods
- Raid defense and siege mechanics
- Stockpile economy and resource management
- Medieval social dynamics among colonists
