# Manor Lords

## Overview

- **Developer**: Grzegorz "Greg" Styczyn (Slavic Magic) — solo developer
- **Publisher**: [[Hooded Horse]]
- **Release**: April 2024 (Early Access)
- **Genre**: Medieval City Builder, Real-Time Tactics
- **Metacritic**: 82/100
- **Sales**: Over 2 million copies in first week, estimated $60M+ gross

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Organic, road-based city building with burgage plots
- [[Manor]] system as the seat of regional power
- Feudal taxation and regional economy management
- Serf/[[peasant]] workforce management
- Trade routes between settlements
- Real-time tactical combat with levied armies
- Seasonal agricultural cycles

## Design Significance

- Most-wishlisted game on Steam prior to release (2M+ wishlists)
- Remarkable solo development achievement
- Proven that visual/authentic approach to feudalism resonates with massive audience

**What to steal**: Visual city growth, organic placement, community transparency
**What to avoid**: Minimal [[feudal relationship]] simulation
