# Medieval Dynasty

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Render Cube]]
- **Publisher**: Toplitz Productions
- **Release**: September 2021 (EA/1.0 PC, later consoles)
- **Genre**: Survival, RPG, City Builder
- **Metacritic**: 74/100 (PC)
- **Sales**: Over 1.5 million copies during EA phase alone

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Settlement founding from scratch in medieval Europe
- Villager management with job assignments and housing
- Dynasty and lineage system — passing rule to heirs
- Medieval economy — farming, hunting, crafting, trade
- Land use and territory expansion
- Technology progression through medieval eras

## Design Significance

**What to steal**: First-person immersion, dynasty mechanics, tactile life simulation
**What to avoid**: Repetitive survival grind

The "medieval life" fantasy is underserved — players want to *live* in a medieval world, not just manage it abstractly.
