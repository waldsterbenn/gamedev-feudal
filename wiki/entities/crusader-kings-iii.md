# Crusader Kings III

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Paradox Development Studio]]
- **Publisher**: Paradox Interactive
- **Release**: September 2020 (PC), March 2022 (consoles)
- **Genre**: Grand Strategy RPG
- **Metacritic**: 91/100 — Universally acclaimed
- **Sales**: Over 3 million copies by September 2023

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- The definitive feudal game — direct simulation of medieval feudalism
- Dynastic succession, inheritance laws, and lineage management
- Feudal contracts with [[vassal|vassals]] — obligations, levies, and taxes
- Social hierarchy from peasant to emperor
- Religion, vassal relations, court intrigue, and marriage alliances
- Decentralized power structure — managing semi-autonomous vassals

## Design Significance

CK3 represents the gold standard for several mechanics:
- **[[Vassal loyalty systems]]**: Multi-layered model (opinion + contracts + dread + factions + power)
- **[[Succession and inheritance]]**: Makes succession the strategic game, not a penalty
- **[[Economy and resource chains]]**: Abstract/strategic approach with gold as universal resource

**What to steal**: Vassal personality system, faction dynamics, succession as gameplay
**What to avoid**: Overwhelming complexity, DLC-heavy model
