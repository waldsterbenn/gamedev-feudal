# Fictiorama Studios

## Overview

- Spanish indie game developer
- Created [[Beloved Rapture]] (2022) — narrative puzzle RPG set during Spanish Inquisition era
- Published by Don't Nod
- Known for bold political and religious themes

## Design Significance

Fictiorama represents the willingness to tackle difficult subjects of feudal theocracy and persecution — a contrast to most feudal games' focus on conquest and building. Shows that narrative-driven feudal experiences can exist alongside mainstream strategy games.
