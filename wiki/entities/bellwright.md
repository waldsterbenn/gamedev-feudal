# Bellwright

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Donkey Crew]]
- **Publisher**: Donkey Crew
- **Release**: April 2024 (Early Access)
- **Genre**: Survival, Action RPG, Base Building
- **Metacritic**: Mostly positive user reviews
- **Sales**: Strong early access sales, trending positively on Steam

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Player leads a rebellion against oppressive feudal lords
- Village building and settlement management
- NPC loyalty and morale systems
- Militia recruitment and training
- Resource gathering and medieval economy
- Direct confrontation with feudal power structures

## Design Significance

Represents the "rebellion" side of the player role split — most feudal games cast the player as lord/Bellwright casts them as challenger. Engaging rebellion narrative and base-building combination.
