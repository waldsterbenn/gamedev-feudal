# Wartales

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Shiro Games]]
- **Publisher**: Shiro Unlimited
- **Release**: April 2023 (Full Release, EA since 2021)
- **Genre**: Tactical RPG, Open World
- **Metacritic**: 80/100
- **Sales**: Over 1 million copies sold

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Mercenary company management in a low-fantasy medieval world
- Feudal contracts — taking jobs from lords, merchants, and nobles
- Regional economy with supply and demand
- Camp hierarchy and retinue management
- Prisoner management and ransom system
- Settlement interactions and guild relations

## Design Significance

Highly praised for tactical combat depth, open-world freedom, and engaging mercenary management. The feudal contract system offers a bottom-up perspective on feudal power relationships.
