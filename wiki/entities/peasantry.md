# Peasantry (Laboratores)
- **Role**: Economic foundation (material sustenance).
- **Sub-groups**:
    - **Freemen**: Legally free, paid rent, some freedom of movement.
    - **Serfs (Villeins)**: Bound to land (not individuals), owed labor (demesne) and fees (milling, baking).
- **Demographics**: 60-90% of total population.
