# Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[TaleWorlds Entertainment]]
- **Publisher**: TaleWorlds Entertainment / Prime Matter
- **Release**: March 2020 (Early Access), October 2022 (Full Release)
- **Genre**: Strategy, Action RPG
- **Metacritic**: 78/100
- **Sales**: Largest Steam launch of 2020 (~250,000 concurrent players). Over 3.6 million copies sold as of 2023.

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Clan-based hierarchy and [[vassal]]age system
- [[Fief]]dom management — granting and administering land, villages, and castles
- Kingdom politics — swearing fealty, waging war, negotiating peace
- Mercenary economy and warband management
- Companion system with loyalty and morale mechanics
- Settlement production chains and taxation

## Design Significance

**What to steal**: Fiefdom management, companion system, tactical combat
**What to avoid**: Single-axis relationship system

Spent 10 years in development — a cautionary tale for scope management.
