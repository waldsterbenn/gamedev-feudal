# Eremite Games

## Overview

- Small development team
- Best known for: [[Against the Storm]] (2023, 500K+ EA sales, "Overwhelmingly Positive" 95%+)
- Genre: City Builder + Roguelite hybrid
- Published by [[Hooded Horse]]

## Design Significance

Demonstrated that innovation in mechanical combination creates defensibility against competitors. Their roguelite + colony builder hybrid was unique in the market. Published by Hooded Horse, showing the publisher's role in indie success.
