# Clergy (Oratores)
- **Role**: Spiritual life, moral salvation.
- **Power**: Owned 20-30% of productive land; exercised significant temporal (secular) power.
- **Organization**: Pope (absolute) > Archbishops > Bishops > Abbots > Parish Priests.
- **Influences**: Owned large monastic estates; involved in investiture disputes (secular vs. divine power appointment).
