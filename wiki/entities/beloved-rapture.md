# Beloved Rapture

## Overview

- **Developer**: Fictiorama Studios (Spain)
- **Publisher**: Don't Nod
- **Release**: September 2022 (EA PC, PS4); 2023 (consoles)
- **Genre**: Narrative Puzzle RPG
- **Metacritic**: Generally favorable reviews
- **Sales**: Modest indie sales; included in several curated indie game collections

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Historical allegory based on Spanish Inquisition era feudal-religious hierarchy
- Church-state power dynamics
- Inquisition-era social control and persecution
- Feudal authority challenged through narrative choices
- Medieval/early modern class tensions
- Faith and power systems

## Design Significance

Noted for tackling difficult subjects of feudal theocracy and persecution. Bold political and religious themes that contrast with most feudal games' focus on conquest and building.
