# Battle Brothers

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Overhype Studios]]
- **Publisher**: Overhype Studios (Windows version later published by [[Hooded Horse]] in 2025)
- **Release**: March 2017 (PC), 2021-2022 (consoles)
- **Genre**: Tactical RPG
- **Metacritic**: 84/100 — Critically acclaimed
- **Sales**: Over 1 million copies across all platforms

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Mercenary company operating in feudal low-fantasy world
- Contracts with nobles, lords, and settlements
- Medieval economy — wages, supplies, equipment maintenance
- Character progression based on medieval skill archetypes
- Faction reputation system with competing feudal powers
- Gritty, realistic medieval combat

## Design Significance

Considered one of the best indie tactical RPGs. Named soldiers create deep emotional investment — the gold standard for army management mechanics. Outstanding sustained player base with continued development.
