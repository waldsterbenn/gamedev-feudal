# TaleWorlds Entertainment

## Overview

- Turkish game developer
- Best known for: [[Mount & Blade]] series — predecessor sold 6M+ copies combined
- [[Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord]] (2020/2022): Largest Steam launch of 2020 (~250K concurrent players), 3.6M+ sold
- Spent 10 years on Bannerlord development — cautionary tale for scope management

## Design Significance

Bannerlord's clan-based hierarchy, fiefdom management, and companion system are strong models for feudal game design. However, the 10-year development cycle warns against scope creep — a key lesson for the [[feudal]] project.
