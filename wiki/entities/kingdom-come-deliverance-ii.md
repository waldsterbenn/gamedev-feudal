# Kingdom Come: Deliverance II

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Warhorse Studios]]
- **Publisher**: Deep Silver
- **Release**: February 4, 2025 (PC, PS5, Xbox Series X/S)
- **Genre**: Action RPG, Open World
- **Metacritic**: Generally favorable reviews
- **Sales**: Sold 2 million copies within two weeks of release

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Expanded feudal politics with deeper noble intrigue
- Estate and land management systems
- Vassal and retainer dynamics
- Enhanced medieval commerce and crafting economy
- Continued strict historical accuracy in Holy Roman Empire setting
