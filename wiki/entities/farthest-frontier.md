# Farthest Frontier

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Crate Entertainment]]
- **Publisher**: Crate Entertainment
- **Release**: August 2022 (Early Access)
- **Genre**: City Builder, Strategy
- **Metacritic**: Mostly positive user reviews (~90%+ on Steam)
- **Sales**: Strong sales for an indie city builder from the Grim Dawn developers

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Medieval frontier settlement building
- Production chains reflecting feudal economy
- Class-based citizen tiers (settlers to nobility)
- Agricultural cycles and land management
- Defense against raiders
- Trade and taxation systems
