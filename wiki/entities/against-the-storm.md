# Against the Storm

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Eremite Games]]
- **Publisher**: [[Hooded Horse]] (PC)
- **Release**: December 2023 (Full Release, EA since 2021)
- **Genre**: City Builder, Roguelite
- **Metacritic**: 85/100 — Won multiple awards
- **Sales**: Over 500,000 copies during EA alone

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Player serves as a feudal [[vassal]] to the Scourgestorm Queen
- Establishing vassal settlements across multiple maps
- Class-based worker economy (Beavers, Harpies, Foxmen, Humans, Lizards)
- Feudal taxation — settlements pay taxes/resources to the queen
- Prestige system reflecting feudal rank
- Resource chains and trade hub management

## Design Significance

**What to steal**: Roguelite structure creating replayability, short meaningful runs
**What to avoid**: Abstracted characters (race-based workers with no names)

Achieved "Overwhelmingly Positive" (95%+) through exceptional Early Access management.
