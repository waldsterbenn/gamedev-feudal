# Nobility (Bellatores)
- **Class**: Dukes, Counts/Earls, Barons, Knights.
- **Role**: Military protection, regional administration, justice administration.
- **Relationship**: Vassalage to King or higher lord; received land (fief) in exchange for service.
- **Cultural Code**: Chivalry (loyalty, protection of weak, courtly behavior).
