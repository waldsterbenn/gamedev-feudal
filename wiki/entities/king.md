# King (Suzerain/Monarch)
- **Role**: Supreme landowner in theory; holds power from God (divine right) or through historical conquest.
- **Powers**: Grants fiefs, summons armies, dispenses highest justice, mints coins.
- **Constraints**: Often limited by powerful barons (witness Magna Carta).
- **Revenue**: Crown lands, feudal aids, reliefs, scutage.
