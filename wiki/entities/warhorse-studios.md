# Warhorse Studios

## Overview

- Czech game developer
- Best known for: [[Kingdom Come: Deliverance]] (2018, 3M+ copies) and [[Kingdom Come: Deliverance II]] (2025, 2M copies in two weeks)
- Speciality: Strictly historical medieval simulation with no fantasy elements
- Crowdfunded KCD via Kickstarter ($1.1M raised in 2014)

## Design Significance

Warhorse Studios sets the bar for historical authenticity in medieval games. Their approach of starting players at the bottom of the [[feudal hierarchy]] as commoners provides a unique perspective on feudal structures that most games don't offer.
