# Songs of Conquest

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Lavapotion]]
- **Publisher**: Coffee Stain Publishing
- **Release**: May 2022 (EA), May 2024 (Full Release)
- **Genre**: Turn-based Strategy
- **Metacritic**: 79/100
- **Sales**: Strong indie strategy game sales

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Four unique fantasy factions with feudal governance
- Territory expansion and kingdom management
- Lord (Wielder) commanding armies and regions
- Resource management and settlement development
- Inspired by 90s turn-based classics with feudal themes
- Conquest and diplomacy systems
