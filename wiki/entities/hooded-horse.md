# Hooded Horse

## Overview

- Gaming publisher that has emerged as a specialist in medieval/strategy indie games
- Published [[Manor Lords]] (PC), which grossed an estimated $60M+ in its first week
- Published [[Battle Brothers]] (Windows version, 2025)
- Published [[Against the Storm]] (PC)
- Known for strong community management and transparent developer relations

## Design Significance

Hooded Horse represents what a focused publishing strategy can achieve in the medieval/strategy space. Their portfolio demonstrates the commercial viability of feudal-themed indie games.
