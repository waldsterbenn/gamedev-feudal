# Kingdom Two Crowns

## Overview

- **Developer**: Thomas van den Berg (Noio) / [[Coatsink]]
- **Publisher**: Raw Fury
- **Release**: December 2018 (PC, Consoles, Mobile)
- **Genre**: Strategy, Side-scrolling, Minimalist
- **Metacritic**: 81/100 — Best entry in the Kingdom series
- **Sales**: Strong indie sales across all platforms

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Monarch as ruler building and defending a kingdom
- Subject recruitment and role assignment
- Feudal defense — walls, archers, and castle upgrades
- Night-cycle siege defense mechanics
- Minimalist feudal hierarchy (monarch > subjects)
- Co-op multiplayer for dual monarchs
