# Ancestors Legacy

## Overview

- **Developer**: [[Destructive Creations]] (Poland)
- **Publisher**: 1C Company
- **Release**: May 2018 (PC), August 2019 (Xbox One, PS4), June 2020 (Switch)
- **Genre**: Real-Time Strategy
- **Metacritic**: 68/100
- **Sales**: Moderate sales, primarily on Steam

## Key Feudal Mechanics

- Multiple medieval factions (Vikings, Anglo-Saxons, Germans, Slavs)
- Clan-based warfare and raiding
- Territorial conquest and village building
- Historical campaigns based on medieval conflicts
- Squad-level tactical combat within RTS framework
- Resource-based economy tied to territory control
