# World & Lore Bible

## Overview

The World & Lore Bible is the master document for the game world's geography, history, culture, and social structure. Status: **Draft** as of 2026-04-05.

## Document Structure

The world lore bible covers these major areas:

### World Overview
- Geography, history, and overall feel of the game world
- To be defined by the design team

### The [[Feudal System]]
Detail how the feudal hierarchy works in the game:
- **[[King]] / Ruler** — The supreme authority
- **Lords / Nobles** — Regional power holders ([[duke]], [[count]], [[baron]])
- **[[Knight]]s / Vassals** — The military and landed class
- **Peasantry** — The productive majority (see [[peasants]])
- **Outlaws / Rebels** — Those outside the feudal order

### Factions
A templated faction system tracking:
- Faction name and motto
- Leadership
- Territory
- Inter-faction relations

### Timeline / History
Key historical events organized by era:
- **Ancient Era** — Early world history
- **Medieval Era** — Rise and peak of feudal structures
- **Present Day** — Current game world state

### Key Locations
Individual location entries with:
- Region
- Description and visual style
- Importance to story

### Culture & Society
- Customs & traditions
- Religion & beliefs
- Economy & trade
- Law & justice

## Status

This document is currently in **Draft** status with placeholder templates. The actual world lore content will be built iteratively through the design process, informed by the [[feudal-system-md|historical feudal system research]].
