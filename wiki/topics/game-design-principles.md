# Game Design Principles

Guidance for game-specific simulation logic and high-level strategy for `gamedev-feudal`.

## Simulation Design
- **Tension**: Central tension between *Demesne* (direct control) and *Vassals* (indirect/feudal management).
- **Hierarchy Simulation**: Implement the "Three Estates" model (Those who pray, Those who fight, Those who work) as the logical basis for NPC behaviors and AI logic.
- **Scalability**: UI must bridge the gap between grand-strategic taxation/territory control and tactile unit/building management.
