## Part-Time Development Workflow

For hobby-level development with evenings and weekends, the recommended approach is:

- **Cap weekday sessions** at 1-2 hours
- **Use weekends** for longer focus blocks (3-4 hours max)
- **Plan for 10-15 productive hours/week/team**, not 20
- **Rotate roles weekly** (code vs design vs pipeline) to avoid fatigue

## Burnout Prevention

Treating development as a protected hobby, not a second job, is critical for long-term sustainability:

- Decouple learning from production -- separate tutorial sessions from shipping sessions
- Enforce hard boundaries: no crunch, no guilt for missed weeks
- Take planned hiatuses (2-4 weeks) when motivation dips
- Track outcomes, not hours
- Ship one small playable piece per week

## Burnout Signals to Watch

- Dreading development sessions
- Refactoring instead of shipping
- Ignoring [[QA Strategy|playtest]] feedback
- Working past planned cut-off time

## Motivation Maintenance

- Build a visible, playable milestone by week 6
- Break work into deliverables
- Ship a mini-build every 2 weeks
- Accept scope reduction as a feature, not a failure
