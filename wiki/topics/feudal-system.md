# Feudal System

Socio-political-economic system based on land (fiefs) in exchange for service/labor. Dominant in medieval Europe (9th–15th centuries).

## Core Pillars
- **Land (Fief)**: Power rests in land.
- **Reciprocal Obligation**: Two-way contract; lord protects, vassal serves.
- **Hierarchy**: Rigidly stratified.
